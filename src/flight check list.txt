1. Get Icecast online by opening RadioBroadcaster, check box in list to connect.
2. Open OBS, hit start streaming check box, manage broadcast, hit Save and start streaming button to go online.
3. Go to YouTube, Your Videos tab, choose video in table, expand details, then search page for Video Link.
4. Either copy link and paste into the imbed section of live.html in the reaper-stream-website repo, or click link, hit share button when video loads, hit Imbed, then go down to Copy button a few lines below check boxes.
5. Go to src folder of repo, rename live.html to index.html, git commit -a and then git push.
