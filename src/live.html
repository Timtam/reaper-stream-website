If you've got a microphone hooked up (or if your device has one in-built), you can also hop on TeamTalk with us in three easy-ish steps. Here's how:

  1. If you're using Windows or Mac, Download and install TeamTalk
  2. If you're on a mobile device, search your Appstore for TeamTalk.
  3. Once you've got TeamTalk installed, Click here to get a TT file that'll drop you straight into the channel with us<./a>
